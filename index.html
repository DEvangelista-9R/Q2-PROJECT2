Bicol Express

Instructions: In a pan, heat oil while saut√©ing the onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. Add the sliced pork belly and cook until browned. Stir in shrimp paste and green/red chillies and cook for another 2 minutes. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for 10-15 minutes until the pork is tender and the sauce thickens. Add shrimp, season with salt and pepper, and cook until shrimp is pink (about 5 minutes).

Chicharron

Instructions: Cut the pork into 1-2 inch squares. Simmer pork in water with salt (and baking soda, if using) for 45 minutes until tender. Drain and pat pork dry completely. Heat oil in a deep pan, fry pork until golden and crispy.

Description

Sorbetes (Dirty Ice Cream)

Instructions: Combine 2 cups coconut milk, 2 cups evaporated milk, 1 cup condensed milk, 1/2 cup sugar, and 1 tsp vanilla extract. Add flavoring (e.g., mango, ube, chocolate) in a pot and stir well. Over low heat, stir until slightly thickened and let cool. Pour into an ice cream maker and churn until creamy. If no ice cream maker, freeze in a container, stirring every hour for 4-6 hours. Transfer to a container and freeze until firm.
